RANKS (Tiers)

Sologuard is all about helping Freelancers leverage their skills. We seek to empower top performing Freelancers with helpful tools to grow their business. Freelancers who invest in self-promotion may achieve greater customer satisfaction. And, if they deliver on time and maintain high quality and ratings, Sologuard may reward them with new statuses, special opportunities, benefits, and tools that come with it.

  • Sologuard Freelancers can gain account Tiers based on their activity, performance and reputation. The advancement criteria can be found  here .

  • Advancement in Tiers are updated periodically by an automated system.

  • The current Tiers a seller can achieve are, Level 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 and Top Rated.

  • Sellers who cannot maintain their high-quality service, experience a severe drop in ratings, or stop delivering on time risk losing their Freelancer status and the benefits that come with it. For example, late deliveries, warnings to the Freelancer’s account and cancellations can cause a Freelancer to move to a different Tier.

  • Advanced Tiers provide their owners with additional benefits, including offering the completion of Tickets for higher prices.

Top Rated Sellers

  • Top Rated Freelancers are chosen manually by Sologuard editors through an ongoing review process based on seniority, volume of ticket completions, extremely high vouch points, exceptional customer care, high order completion rate and community leadership. Top Rated Freelancers gain access to more extensive features than previous tiers, including exclusive access to beta features and VIP support.

  • Top Rated eligibility is constantly evaluated by Sologuard to ensure that the quality standards and expectations of the Top-Rated selection is kept. Sologuard retains the right to change a Top-Rated Freelancer status in light of such evaluation. In addition, Top Rated Freelancers who cannot maintain their high-quality service through a severe drop in ratings, stop delivering on time, increased cancellation rate or violate our Terms of Service, also risk losing their Top-Rated status and the benefits that come with it.


Pro Freelancers

  • Pro Freelancers are pre-verified professionals who undergo a vetting process by Sologuard editors. Applying for Sologuard Pro is open to everyone: professional freelancers who are new to Sologuard, as well as existing Sologuard Freelancers.

Reviews

  • Feedback reviews provided by Clients while completing an order are an essential part of Sologuard’s rating system (Vouch Points). Vouch Points demonstrate the Client’s overall experience with the Freelancer’s and their service. Clients are encouraged to communicate to the Freelancer any concerns experienced during their active order in regards to the service provided by the Freelancer.

  • Leaving a Client’s feedback is a basic prerogative of a Client. Feedback reviews (Vouch Points) will not be removed unless there are clear violations to our Terms of Service.

  • To prevent any misuse of our Feedback system, all feedback reviews must come from legitimate sales executed exclusively through the Sologuard platform from users within our Community. Purchases arranged, determined to artificially enhance seller ratings, or to abuse the Sologuard platform with purchases from additional accounts, will result in a permanent suspension of all related accounts.

  • Feedback comments given by Clients are publicly accessible on a Freelancers account. Clients have the option not to include a comment, but still rate the service.

  • Work Samples are the delivered images and videos sent to a buyer in a delivery message. Work Samples are added to a Freelancer’s Live Portfolio on their profile if the buyer chooses to publish the Work Sample while providing a public feedback review.

  • Withholding the delivery of services, files, or information required to complete the Ticket’s service with the intent to gain favorable reviews or additional services is prohibited.

  • Responding and posting a review: Once work is delivered, the buyer has three days to respond and post a review (or 14 days for Tickets with shipping). If no response is provided within the response period, the order will be considered completed.

  • Users are allowed to leave reviews on orders up to 30 days after an order is marked as complete. No new reviews may be added to an order after 30 days.

  • Freelancers may not solicit the removal of feedback reviews from their Clients through mutual cancellations.

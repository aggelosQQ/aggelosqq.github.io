ActivateMC Hosting Privacy Policy

Personal Information

Any personal information you provide for use on ActivateMCHosting.com will not be given or distributed to any outside parties for any purpose. Any information that we are provided is for strictly only used on ActivateMCHosting.com

How do we protect your information?

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or access your personal information. Some user data is stored in your browser cookies. However, never, under any circumstances, is any sensitive data stored in your browser cookies. All client data that has been inputted is stored in our databases (username, password, IP address, etc) although all sensitive information (such as passwords) is automatically encrypted and cannot be viewed by ActivateMC Hosting staff. Other private information, such as credit card numbers and security questions, aren’t stored in our databases and won’t ever be in the future either.

Third party sites

  1. We may include or offer third party products or services on our website. These third party sites have separate and have other independent privacy policies. We therefore hold no liability or responsibility for the content and activities of those separate websites.
  2. We do not give away, sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any personal identifiable information to any third parties. Although we may release partial information when we believe release is appropriate, this includes situations: with the law, to enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety from personal or bodily harm in accordance governed by UK, Scotland and Wales laws.

Online Privacy Policy Only

This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to information collected offline.

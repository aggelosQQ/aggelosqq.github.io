Who are we?

ActivateMC Hosting is a british business founded by Leo Hall in early January 2017. ActivateMC Hosting was founded on certain beliefs that there was a gap in the market when it comes to affordable hosting with dedicated support when you need it - nobody wants to wait days for a response. We pride ourselves on maintaining a quality service whilst holding and offering an affordable and competitive price. At ActivateMC Hosting we have really strong motos and passion and we aim to provide the very best for the Minecraft community, this includes offering a cheaper alternative which maintains industry standard hardware and cutting edge technology to keep everything moving smoothly.
